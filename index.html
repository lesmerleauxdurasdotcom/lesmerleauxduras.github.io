More about Les Merleaux

Les Merleaux is a traditional French country house nestled on the edge of the picturesque hamlet of Lubersac. It's tranquil location in the heart of the Aquitane region is ideal for a relaxing holiday.

Les Merleaux is conveniently located just a five minute drive from the market town of Duras, with it's stunning castle, traditional Monday market  and numerous shops, cafés, bars and restaurants. Within a short drive are many towns and villages, many with their own day and night markets.  Slightly further afield are the towns of Sainte-Foy-la-Grande, Eymet and Marmande.

The accommodation itself is of a high standard and comfortably accommodates 11 guests. It is suitable for large families, family groups or couples. With five bedrooms and four bathrooms there is plenty of space to accommodate your group. In addition there is a fully equipped kitchen  with seating for eight plus a breakfast bar.

A large, comfortable lounge with a flat screen TV and UK channels via satellite, a separate dining area with a table to seat eight and a play room complete the interior.

The generous proportions of Les Merleaux extend to the outdoor space. The kitchen opens out onto a half an acre garden with patio and seating for your meals al fresco.  The private pool area with sunbeds to relax on and two additional seating areas, is another space you will find where you can unwind and enjoy your time at Les Merleaux.
