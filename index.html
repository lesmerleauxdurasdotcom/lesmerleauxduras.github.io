Les Merleaux, a traditional French country house with five bedrooms, its own private pool and Wi-Fi, is available for holiday lettings all year round.

This traditional French country house with five bedrooms is of a high standard and comfortably accommodates eleven guests. It is suitable for large families, family groups or couples. With five bedrooms and four bathrooms there is plenty of space.

Facilities include a fully equipped kitchen with seating for eight and a separate breakfast bar. A large comfortable lounge with a flat screen TV and UK channels via satellite leads to a separate dining area with a table to seat eight. A play room, along with the five bedrooms and four bathrooms, complete the interior of this traditional French country house. Wi-Fi connected to hi-speed internet is available.

The generous proportions of this traditional French country house extend to the outdoor space. The kitchen opens out onto a half an acre garden with patio and seating for your meals al fresco. The private pool area with sunbeds to relax on and two additional seating areas, is another space you will find where you can unwind and enjoy your time at this beautiful traditional French country house.

Nestled on the edge of the picturesque hamlet of Lubersac, this traditional French country house with five bedrooms tranquil location in the heart of Lot-et-Garonne, Aquitane, south west France is ideal for a holiday relaxing by the private pool.

Les Merleaux is conveniently located just a five minute drive from the market town of Duras, with it's stunning castle, traditional Monday market and numerous shops, cafés, bars and restaurants. Within a short drive are many towns and villages, many with their own day and night markets. Slightly further afield are the towns of Sainte-Foy-la-Grande, Eymet and Marmande.
